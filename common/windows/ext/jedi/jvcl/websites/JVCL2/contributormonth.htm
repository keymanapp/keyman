JVCL Contributors of the Month

JVCL cannot exist without involvement of JEDI members, and on this page we would like to acknowledge the most significant contributions. 

While the title of this page suggests a "monthly" cycle, these awards will be presented as we see outstanding contributions to JVCL, i.e. there could more than one award in a month, or the could be none. One thing is for sure - those people who are recognized did some really cool and outstanding things for JVCL, and we truly appreciate it!

February 2004 Andreas Hausladen and André Snepvangers For their amazing work on VisualCLX support in JVCL.
February 2003 Ralf Grenzing For his work on JVCL Mega-Demo

b) for constant bug fixes and improvements to JVCL
February 2003 Robert Marquardt For restructuring/clean-up of JVCL.
August 2002 Petr Vones For creation of "JEDI Installer" making installation of JCL and JVCL a one-click event.
August 2002 Remko Bonte For his conversion of Help from 1.31 to 2.0, and for several bug fixes.
July 2002 Peter Thörnqvist For his work on restructuring of JVCL, and making JVCL 2.0 becoming a reality.
July 2002 Sebastien Buysse For writing a Gateway between JVCL newsgroups and mailing lists. Sebastien was also the one who donated over 200 components to JVCL, and his continuing support and involvement in JVCL is outstanding. 
June 2002 Matthias Thoma For providing "Issue Tracker" for JVCL - our Bug tracking database, and merging it with JCL "Issue Tracker" so now we can have one database for all JEDI-related bugs/wishes 
June 2002 Marcel Bestebroer  For taking the leadership in writing Help file for JVCL, and his commitment and perseverance in achieving it.  
June 2002 Andrei Prygounkov For making JVCL 1.31 compatible with Delphi 5, and for donating RALib to JVCL
